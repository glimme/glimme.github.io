Okay, so a long, long time ago (inagalaxyfaraway no, sorry), there was a king and queen with no kids. This made the queen sad, so she
went outside to sew, in the dead of winter, because what else would you do? So she was sitting there with her nice ebony sewing hoop
thing (frame?), in the snow, when she pricked herself with the needle, and a drop of blood fell on the snow. Instead of being in pain,
she thought that color combination looked really pretty, and wished for a child ‘as dark as ebony, as white as snow, and as red as blood.’
Then her wish came true, because this is a fairytale, and you have to be careful what you wish for. Fairies are tricky. Then she died,
I think in childbirth, because, again, fairytale.
This made the king very sad, so he got married to the most beautiful woman in the land. He is now irrelevant.
Now, the woman he married was now a step-mother, as well as a queen. By fairytale law, she was now the most evil being in the land. And
she was very vain. She looked good, knew it, and made damn sure everyone else knew it. She had this huge mirror in her room, and every
day she went up to this mirror and asked it for validation. And every day it assured her she was the most beautiful.
Until one day (because who wants a story about the status quo not breaking down), the mirror replied that Snow White had
become more beautiful than her overnight, somehow.
